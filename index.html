Recommended A/B Testing Framework

Before full deployment of pricing changes, validate with controlled experiments

Test 1: Club Pricing

Hypothesis: Raising Club prices by 25% will not significantly reduce demand

Control: Current $120/game pricing

Treatment: $150/game pricing

Metric: Conversion rate, total revenue

Test 2: Early-Bird Incentive

Hypothesis: 10% discount for 30+ day purchases will shift demand earlier

Control: Standard pricing timeline

Treatment: 10% early-bird discount

Metric: % sales 30+ days out

Test 3: Personalized Timing

Hypothesis: Sending promos aligned with buyer type increases conversion

Control: Generic email blast to all

Treatment: Targeted emails by segment

Metric: Open rate, click rate, conversion

Why A/B Test First? While immediate deployment of pricing changes captures revenue faster, A/B testing ensures the model works in real-world conditions and builds organizational confidence in data-driven decisions.

Recommendation: Run Tests 1 and 2 for the first half of next season, then full deployment if successful.
